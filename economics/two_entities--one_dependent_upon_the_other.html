Two entities — one dependent upon the other

Model

  1. Two entities: F and T.
  2. F ('farmer') produces a product P_F.
  3. T ('technician') produces a product P_T in single quantities.
  4. P_F is necessary for T's survival in quantity S.
  5. P_T is not necessary for F's survival.
  6. P_F can be delivered to T in quantity Q at intervals of duration D_F.
  7. P_T can be delivered to F at intervals of duration D_T.

Stability

Stability (permanent survival of T) can occur only under the following conditions:

  1. Repeated delivery of P_T is desirable for F.
  2. Q is greater than or equal to S.
  3. D_T is less than or equal to D_F.

Verdict

Survival of T is extremely fragile, dependent upon the whim of F: the desirability of P_T for F cycle after cycle, as well as F's willingness and ability to produce P_F for T in sufficiently high quantities.

Variants

  1. If, instead of one 'farmer' F, there are n 'farmers', condition 1. for stability can be relaxed from desirability of repeated delivery for every cycle of the model to desirability of repeated delivery every n cycles. T's survival over a greater number of model cycles is more likely, but T's ultimate survival remains fragile.
  2. Condition 1. for survival can be circumnavigated if T is able to produce a new product in single quantities for each cycle ('innovation'), each of which is desirable for F. The fragility of T's survival is not significantly lessened.
  3. If D_T is significantly less than D_F, greater flexibility is possible with regard to the respective deliveries of P_F and P_T (e.g. one delivery of P_F might be made in the same cycle as two deliveries of P_T, whilst in the next cycle a delivery of P_F might be made with no delivery of P_T), but the model is not significantly altered.

Effect of regulation

  1. F could be required through regulation to produce P_F in quantity S at intervals of duration D_F. This would remove the whim of F from consideration for the survival of T. The ability of F to produce P_F in sufficient quantities in time is unlikely to be able to regulated, however.
