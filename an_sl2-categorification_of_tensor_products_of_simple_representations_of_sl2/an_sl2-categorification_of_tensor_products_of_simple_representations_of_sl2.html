An sl2-categorification of tensor products of simple representations of sl2

2008

Transfer thesis

pdf dvi tex bib

Abstract

Hao Zheng gave a geometric categorification of tensor products of simple U_q(sl_2)-modules. We extend his work to a 2-categorical setting, in line with the higher representation theory programme of Rouquier.
