Information

  • September 2017 – present. Progamvareutvikler (software developer), SINTEF Ocean, Trondheim, Norway.
  • January 2016 – August 2017. Software engineer, Telenor Digital, Trondheim, Norway. Principally worked on development of, and led operational support for, HTTP APIs for SMS, backed by SMPP and (indirectly) SS7.
  • August 2012 – July 2015. Postdoktor i matematikk (postdoc in mathematics), Geometry and Topology group, NTNU, Norway.
  • January 2012 – July 2012. Gjesteforsker (guest researcher), Geometry and Topology group, NTNU, Norway, at the invitation of Nils Baas.
