Literary Chinese

易經

The characters of the text are presented in the traditional way: in vertical lines (to be read from top to bottom), proceeding from right to left. Punctuation is not included, as it was either not present or only very lightly present in the source manuscripts. Every character is a link to a page for that character. Romanisations in the front matter are according to the Chinese phonetic alphabet (Hanyu Pinyin).
