Waves

Dedicated to my Mum and Dad on the occasion of their 43rd wedding anniversary

Etched upon the water, four straight lines:
pegmatite veins within rock.
Wind-without at morning, five hours' time:
froth of a gentle tide's swell.

Written on the morning of the 26th of July 2023, looking out over the same bay at Flakstad in Lofoten as when I wrote Disturbance. Inspired by waves observed there the previous night, as well as the landscape of Leka.
