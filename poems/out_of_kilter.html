Out of kilter

Jupiter's displaced the moon
and leftmost one star of Orion's belt
ruddies brighter than the other two.

Streetlight-less once passed the bridge,
this twenty-third hour of a working day
thickens sour as with the dark of six.

Miles through pipes away and out,
an opening tap fills a night-owl's glass
metres down from where the road here prowls.
