Most of my research from the last 6 years or so has not been written up in a final form or made publically available. This includes a considerable amount of work that was carried out upon demonstrating that the homotopy hypothesis holds, in a strong sense, for certain kinds of cubical higher groupoids, and work related to this that was carried out in the foundations of homotopy theory, some of which built upon my thesis.

For the last few years, I have mainly been working on diagrammatic knot theory and 3-manifold theory. This work should see the light of day soon.
