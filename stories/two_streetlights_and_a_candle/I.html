I

Curiously enough, in this town of ours there are two old streetlights, a considerable distance apart even to one accustomed to walking, in all its capriciousness, the shortest way arranged by fate, time, and a dash of human interference between them. Of cast iron, they are ornately, one might even say delicately, constructed; yet are so much alike, had one set them side by side, as to defeat the most earnest insistence upon a difference between them.

This is the first part of an episodic tale begun in September 2023.
