Miscellaneous

Recordings

Some recordings (simply done, on a mobile phone!) of myself accompanied by a guitar or keyboard. Mostly old folk or roots songs, but occasionally dipping into other things. The full list can be found here. Some examples are the following (in chronological order of my recording them!):

  • Lock the door, Lariston (old Scottish song written by James Hogg)
  • That lucky old sun (Tin Pan Alley classic)
  • La marche nuptiale (George Brassens)
  • Hughie the Graeme (ancient Scottish ballad)
  • Who's the fool now? (English folk song)
  • Western hobo (Carter family roots song)
  • Lord Marlborough (English broadside)
  • Jock o' Braidislee (another ancient Scottish ballad)
  • Fola, fola blakken (Norwegian children's song)
  • O come, o come, Emmanuel (Christmas carol)
  • Roving blade (American/Irish/British folk song)
  • Days of '49 (American folk song)
  • Three hours (Nick Drake)

Stories

A couple of stories, originally intended to be children's stories, but perhaps not quite ending up as such: The giant, the little girl, and the butterfly, and The forest clock.

Poetry

A translation from Norwegian to English of the poem On the night sea (På natthavet) by Kolbein Falkeid, as well as of I envy (Jeg misunner) by the same author.
